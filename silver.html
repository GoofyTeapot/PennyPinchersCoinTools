U.S. Silver Coins

Silver coins are some of the most collected U.S. coins due to their metal value, history, and iconic designs. From Mercury dimes to Walking Liberty halves, silver coins remain a core part of American numismatics.

Key Silver Coins

  • Morgan Dollar (1878–1921) — iconic and highly collected
  • Peace Dollar (1921–1935) — last circulating silver dollar
  • Walking Liberty Half (1916–1947) — considered one of the most beautiful U.S. coins
  • Mercury Dime (1916–1945) — popular with collectors
  • Barber Series (1892–1916) — dime, quarter, half

How to Identify a Silver Coin

  • Pre‑1965 dimes, quarters, and halves are 90% silver
  • 1965–1970 halves are 40% silver
  • Silver coins have a “ring” when dropped on wood
  • Edges are solid silver (no copper stripe)
  • Weight is higher than clad coins
